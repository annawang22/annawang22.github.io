# Simplified Website Revamp SPEC

## Core Goal
Make the website feel:
- clean
- calm
- curated
- visually cohesive

Focus ONLY on:
1. Removing clutter
2. Improving visual design

---

## Phase 1 — Cleanup

### Objective
Remove unnecessary, unfinished, or cluttered content.

### Tasks
- Remove ALL instances of "TBD"
- Remove placeholder sections or cards
- Delete unfinished or low-quality content
- Reduce overly dense sections
- Keep only strongest, most important content

### Guidelines
- Homepage should feel curated, not crowded
- Avoid long lists or walls of text
- Prefer short previews over full detail

### Done When
- No "TBD" anywhere
- No placeholder content
- Page feels noticeably cleaner
- Content is concise and intentional

---

## Phase 2 — Visual Redesign

### Objective
Make the site visually beautiful, calm, and cohesive.

### Design Direction
- Colors:
  - muted forest green
  - sage green
  - warm cream background
- Style:
  - calm, nature-inspired
  - soft, atmospheric (Studio Ghibli influence)
  - professional, not cartoonish

### Typography
- Serif for headings
- Clean readable body text
- Subtle typewriter-style accents

### Layout
- Spacious with lots of breathing room
- Clear section separation
- Avoid cramped or dense layouts

### Components
- Cards with:
  - soft corners
  - gentle shadows
  - consistent spacing
- Buttons:
  - simple, elegant
  - subtle hover effects

### Motion
- Gentle animations only:
  - soft hover lift
  - smooth transitions
  - no flashy effects

### Done When
- Site feels calm and polished
- Colors are cohesive and soft
- Typography feels intentional
- Layout is clean and spacious
- Interactions feel smooth and subtle

---

## Final Vision
A simple, elegant one-page portfolio that feels:
- calm
- intentional
- warm
- thoughtfully designed

Not crowded. Not flashy. Just clean and beautiful.
